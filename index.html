Food

Ghanaian cuisine offers a unique blend of flavours like no other cuisine in the world. The multitude of dishes this fantastic city brings is enourmous and we are on a mission to help you find the best suited to your palette. We have it all covered in our helpful guide. Find out more about modern day favourites like fried chicken and jollof rice or Tilapia & banku, perfect for fish lovers.

Restaurants

The prime objective to this site is to make your trip easier, and by doing so we have compiled a list of the best restaurants to enjoy these traditional dishes. Our list of recommended restaurants offers tourists the chance to gain a better picture into where the best food places are in town. We have also included our very own personal rating to further aide your decisions when you arrive.

Festivals

Explore the food culture of Accra to even greater lengths by visiting numerous festivals Accra has to offer throughout the year. There are multiple festivals in this city which offer great food and hospitality that are welcome to both locals and tourists. We have you covered with a list of some of the best festivals to visit during your time of stay. Whatever the time is, there is always something on.
